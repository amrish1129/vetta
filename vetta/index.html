Services

  • Structural Engineering

    Innovative thinking and collaborative approach.

    Our structural engineering and building design services caters to a variety of clients including designers, building owners, developers, contractors, manufacturers and architects.

    From preliminary and final design to inspection and renovation, we are experts in the design and analysis retaining walls, industrial and residential buildings, seismic analysis and retrofitting.

    We also maintain a close working relationship with the geotechnical engineer to analyze the soil and structure interaction and incorporate the findings into the final design.


  • Project Management

    Comprehensive supervision, inspection and commissioning services for every phase of your construction project.

    Our project management services provide you with comprehensive supervision, inspection and commissioning services for all types of projects – no matter the size or location.

    We offer you extensive experience in all aspects of construction. As a result, we can help you reduce technical risks, prevent construction errors, control budgets and effectively keep your construction project within the planned schedule.

    We provide public institutions, private investors, real estate owners and construction companies with trusted project management.


  • Structural Integrity Management

    Fresh perspective and proven solutions for structural evaluation and repair.

    We understand that competent and effective structural condition and risk assessment and requires a detailed knowledge of the design basis of structures, including loading and resistance parameters. We internally developed and customised structural risk assessment processes that establish the correct context when assessing structural risks.

    These processes can include:
    • Visual inspection techniques coupled with the targeted use of non-destructive testing where appropriate.
    • On-site initial risk assessments that can be performed by our Structural Engineers with the background knowledge to make rapid assessments.
    • Where necessary, techniques such as weighing, strain measurement.

    Helping you to complete your project on time and on budget, while meeting all relevant regulations and quality standards.

    Our independent construction supervision can help guide your project to completion on time, on budget and in compliance with the relevant quality standards.
